About My Family 👪

This is My Family

My family consists of my parents and siblings.

My Parents

My father, Mohd Nazri bin Mohamud, is 48 years old and hails from Batu Kurau, Perak. He is the most inspirational person in my life. He teaches me valuable life values, such as honesty, dedication, and respect for others. He always makes time to help me with my studies and encourages me to achieve my goals. I am grateful to have a father who is not just loving but also my biggest source of strength.

My mother, Fatimah binti Abdullah is 43 years old and hails from Taiping, Perak. She is the heart of our home, ensuring we are always happy and comfortable. She is not only my parent but also my best friend. I love to share my stories and problems with her and she is always there to hear and support me. I am grateful to have her in my life, and I aspire to make her as proud of me as I am of her.

My Siblings

I have 7 siblings, consisting of 3 boys and 4 girls. My siblings are my best friends, bring deep meaning to my days as we share our stories, secrets, and joys together. Although we sometimes argue over small things, our love for each other never fades. I feel truly blessed to have them in my life.
